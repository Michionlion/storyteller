# Beat Prose Generator Prompt

Given the story context and a beat title, write immersive prose
for that beat. Show, don't tell. Include dialogue, sensory details, and
emotional resonance. Return only the prose, no JSON.

## Prompt Guidelines

- Show, don't tell (e.g., "Her hands trembled" not "She was nervous")
- Include sensory details (sights, sounds, smells, textures, tastes)
- Use active voice and strong verbs
- Include character dialogue when appropriate
- Maintain consistent tone and style with the story concept
- Keep paragraphs concise for readability
- End with a sense of forward momentum or emotional impact

## Examples

**Bad:** "She was scared of the dark."
**Good:** "The shadows in the corner pulsed like a heartbeat, and she clutched her flashlight until her knuckles turned white."

**Bad:** "The city was noisy."
**Good:** "Car horns blared like wounded animals, and the scent of roasted chestnuts mixed with exhaust fumes as street vendors hawked their wares."
